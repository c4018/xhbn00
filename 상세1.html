BODY IMMERSE

Released from material substance, digital art allows our bodies to become more immersed in the artwork than ever before. The medium can be transformative and the intentional movement and behavior of people can cause visual changes in the artwork. With immersion of the body into the artwork, the boundary between the self and the artwork becomes ambiguous.
